Haryana State CSR Trust

The Haryana State CSR Trust Board's mission is to coordinate many social projects spread across the state. The Board will direct and indirectly intervene to support and promote corporate social responsibility (CSR) investments made by companies and organizations. It will accomplish this by encouraging collaboration amongst CSR stakeholders in the public sector, private sector, and civil society to build and improve public infrastructure for the good of the populace. Haryana is home to 80% of Fortune 500 companies, hence the potential size of the CSR fund is huge. The board will work to direct this fund by encouraging collaboration among local communities, businesses, NGOs, and the government.
